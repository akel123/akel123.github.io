About Me

My name is Aaron Kelley and I am in my second semester of my master's degree at the Institute of Mathematics of the National Autonomous University of Mexico. While my current research is centered around coexistence in multispecies systems, I am interested in applying this in other contexts as well. Before starting my master's degree, I gained some experience in computational neuroscience to which I am interested in returning to at some point in my scientific career. Additionaly, I am interested in continuing my studies with a PhD after my master's.
